OnePlus 6T was introduced in the Indian markets in October, 2018. As far as the phone’s design is concerned, the matte black Midnight model stands out in its looks and is quite attractive due to its glossy design. The design and display are two good features that come with the OnePlus 6T.

OnePlus 6T is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 845. The phone can literally handle any task thrown at it with ease along with multitasking being super smooth. Modern high-end games with the highest settings do not experience a frame-drop which is a great feature for gamers. The fingerprint sensor is blazing fast and unlocks the phone within 0.34 seconds as per OnePlus. The company has recently introduced the Nightscape mode in this device. This is especially created to click low-light pictures. The inbuilt AI of the phone helps create crisp, low-light images in this mode. The 16MP selfie camera captures enough face details for good selfies which in turn produces social-media worthy selfies. The phone packs a stunning 3,700mAh battery which was surprisingly lowered by only 10% even while watching a HD video on loop with the maximum brightness. This is a great inclusion in the phone that minimizes the battery drainage.
